# DEDCO — BRAND FILM PROMPT (Veo 3 / Runway Gen-4 / Kling)

> Copiez-collez l'intégralité de ce prompt dans votre générateur de vidéo IA.
> Les instructions sont en anglais (meilleure compréhension par les modèles).
> Les textes affichés à l'écran sont en français.

---

## SYSTEM PROMPT

Create a 60-second premium cinematic brand film introducing DEDCO, a contemporary African marketplace dedicated to interior design, furniture, decoration, craftsmanship and interior design services.

The objective is to position DEDCO as the premium destination where inspiration, craftsmanship, design professionals and beautiful spaces come together.

This is NOT a commercial for a furniture store. It is a premium brand manifesto.

The emotional tone should feel like a mix of Apple, Airbnb, Aesop, COS, Ferm Living, IKEA Creative and Pinterest Editorial, adapted to contemporary African design.

---

## VISUAL IDENTITY

Respect the DEDCO visual identity strictly.

Color palette:
- Warm Cream #F7F3ED (primary background)
- Amber #B8702F (accents, highlights)
- Terracotta #9B3D28 (warm focal points)
- Deep Forest Green #4A7A3C (validation, nature)
- Rich Charcoal #1E1813 (text, contrast)

The overall atmosphere must feel warm, refined, contemporary and timeless.

Typography rules for on-screen text:
- Headlines and titles: Use a font that closely resembles "Quache" (a bold, characterful display serif with strong personality, similar to Fraunces or Playfair Display Black).
- Numbers and digits: Use "Plus Jakarta Sans" (clean modern sans-serif) for any numbers displayed on screen. The display font does NOT render numbers — they must fall back to Plus Jakarta Sans.
- Body text: Use "Plus Jakarta Sans" for all secondary text and descriptions.

Minimal layouts. Editorial composition. Elegant spacing. Premium visual hierarchy.

Elegant premium motion graphics. Editorial transitions. Large typography. Minimal UI overlays. Soft masking. Layered compositions. Object tracking. Smooth camera movement. Slow zoom.

No flashy animations. No glitch. No social media style editing. No fast cuts. Every transition should feel luxurious.

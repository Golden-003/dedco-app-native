## TYPOGRAPHY NOTES FOR POST-PRODUCTION

- Headlines font: Search for "Quache" font, or use "Fraunces" (Google Fonts) as the closest free alternative. Weight: Black or Bold.
- Numbers font: "Plus Jakarta Sans" (Google Fonts). Weight: Bold.
- The Quache font EXCLUDES digits (0-9) from its unicode-range. Any number on screen MUST render in Plus Jakarta Sans.
- Text color: Warm cream (#F7F3ED) on dark backgrounds, charcoal (#1E1813) on light backgrounds.
- Accent color for keywords: Amber (#B8702F) or Terracotta (#9B3D28).
